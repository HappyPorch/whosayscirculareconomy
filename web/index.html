Who says "Circular Economy"?

What is this?

I started with this question: "how is the term 'Circular Economy' used in different contexts?"

To explore this question, I'm identifying lists of related websites. Then searching for the term "Circular Economy" on these websites (via a Google API) and see what I find!

But why?

Words matter. The labels we give things affect how we view them and therefore what action they result in. So, I hypothesize that use of specific terms on public websites tells us something!

What does this tell us?

Maybe nothing, other than who uses the term "Circular Economy", but watch this space...
