Who says "Circular Economy"?

[beta]
What is this?

We started with this question: "how is the term 'Circular Economy' used in different contexts?"

As an initial experimental way to explore this question, we are idenitifying lists of websites in specific sectors or other groupings. We then search for the term "Circular Economy" on these wbsites (via a Google API) and see what we find!

But why?

Part of the hypothesis is that names matter. The labels we give things affect how we view them and therefore what action they result in. So, we hypothesise that use of specific terms on public websites tells us something!

What does this tell us?

We don't know yet! Maybe nothing, other than who uses the term "Circular Economy".
